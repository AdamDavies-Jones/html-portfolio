The Best Movies According to Adam

My top 3 anime movies of all-time.

My Neighbor Totoro

This charming yet magical film is a treat for people of all ages! Sasuke and Mei move to a new house, with Sasuke having to start at a new school and their dad working at a University. Straight away, the girls realise they are not alone in their house and certain spirits make themselves known...

Spirited Away

Fantastical and frightening creatures await in this tale about identity and knowing who you are. From the treacherous Yubaba to the terrifyingly hungry No-Face, there are imaginative and creepy spirits abound in this creepy adventure!

Laputa

Known as "Castle in the Sky" in the West, Laputa is a adventurous story about a treasure hidden in the sky. Robots, flying islands, pirates, explosions and vehicle chases all feature in this film, with charming characters along for the ride.
